ABOUT US

Mount Rushmore Unit is a team of dedicated financial advisors under Sun Life Philippines. Established in August 2020 by Unit Manager Jan Timothy C. Sese, the team is composed of professionals from diverse fields, including banking, healthcare, education, and business.

Our Mission

Our purpose as an organization is to help our clients achieve lifetime financial security and live healthier lives. In doing so, we are commited to put our clients at the center of everything we do as we strive to be the best insurance and asset management companies in the world.

Our Vision

Become the trusted financial partner of our clients, making them clients for Life. We will do this by helping them achieve lifetime financial security and live healthier lives through trusted advice, caring service and relevant solutions.

As an outcome we will sustain our market leadership.

Our Objectives

  • Deepen client relationships, measured through increased percentage of clients who own multiple Sun Life products.
  • Embed Client for Life into the organization by transforming our business while developing organization scalability
  • Drive sustainable growth through innovation, digital transformation, and operational excellence

Jan Timothy C. Sese

Mr. Jan Timothy C. Sese serves as the Unit Manager for the Mount Rushmore Unit at Sun Life. Formerly a Bank Teller, he transitioned into the financial industry as a Licensed Financial Advisor and has since demonstrated his dedication and expertise. He was officially coded on August 24, 2018, as a full-time advisor. His journey with Sun Life saw him appointed as Marketing Center (MC) Head on May 1, 2019, and later promoted to Unit Manager on August 1, 2020. Jan Timothy's leadership has been pivotal in driving the success of his team and fostering client satisfaction.
